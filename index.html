About

I'm a second-year Electrical Engineering student at UBC, based in West Vancouver. My interest is in building real hardware — circuits, embedded systems, eventually power systems and sustainable technology. Day-to-day I work mostly in C++ and Python, with KiCad and Arduino for the hardware side.

This site is a live record of how I'm learning. I write about specific problems mid-project, come back after I've solved them, and keep a running log of what's in progress. The goal is to document the actual process, not just the finished result.

I'm actively looking for co-op opportunities and UBC design team positions where I can build things that matter.
